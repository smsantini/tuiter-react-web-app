    "The way a keyboard feels is important to many typists, and the keycaps are a big part of that. Different materials, thicknesses, and profiles can all affect how typing on a keyboard actually feels. For example, thicker or thinner keycaps can change the actuation feel of your switches, depending on what kind of switches you use. And while some materials, like ABS plastic, are smooth, others, like PBT plastic, have a textured surface that can make the keys feel "grippy" under your fingers." https://hhkeyboard.us/blog/mechanical-keyboard-keycaps
